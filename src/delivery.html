Delivery/shipping costs

SHIPPING NATIONWIDE

We ship our products nationwide with GO!, DPD Food and DHL-Express. With Angel Fiege we ship in the Berlin and Potsdam area. To protect your wallet, we cover part of the additional express costs - the reliability of the delivery is worth this luxury for us :-).

Delivery days are from Tuesday to Saturday , after public holidays it takes one day longer. During check-out you can freely select your desired delivery day as soon as you have entered the zip code .

DELIVERY TIMES

Orders received by us by 7 p.m. will be prepared, packaged and handed over to the logistics provider the following day.

The EXPRESS delivery is guaranteed to come “over-night”.
You are always kept up to date on the whereabouts of your package using the GO!, DPD or DHL shipment tracking tracker.

ATTENTION :

If you need our meat on a specific day, it is better to schedule the delivery a day earlier than planned. This way it will definitely be in your fridge in time.

IMPORTANT :

Make sure that our package is accepted on the day of delivery, otherwise it may sit in a pickup station for a day longer. Unfortunately, we cannot accept any liability for any resulting interruption of the cold chain!
