Duck with honey mustard glaze and roasted asparagus: festive Christmas meal for the whole family

As a Michelin-starred chef, it is very important to me to prepare an unforgettable Christmas meal for my family. I attach particular importance to ensuring that everyone gets their money's worth and can indulge in the culinary delights. This year one of my new favorite recipes is on the menu: duck with honey mustard glaze and roasted asparagus. I serve fried asparagus and mashed potatoes as a side dish.
