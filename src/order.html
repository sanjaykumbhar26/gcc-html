ORDER WHERE

You can order on our website around the clock. We are happy to answer telephone orders and questions about the products from Monday to Friday between 9 a.m. and 4 p.m.

ORDER HOW

You put the products in the shopping cart by selecting the desired weight.
When you checkout, you will be asked to enter your desired delivery date.
Then all you have to do is choose your preferred payment method - we'll do the rest for you.
Your cell phone number is important! However, we ONLY need them in the event that there are questions or delays in delivery.

NATIONWIDE DELIVERY

Orders received by us by 7 p.m. will be freshly portioned, packaged and shipped DHL-EXPRESS, DPD, GO! the following day. or hand over to Angel Fiege.

Shipping takes one day. DHL Express, DPD and GO! Deliver reliably, but it can happen that the courier doesn't show up at the door until the evening.

ATTENTION :

If you need our meat by a specific date, it is better to order it a day earlier than planned.
This way it will definitely be in your fridge in time!
