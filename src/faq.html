FAQ – Frequently asked questions

  • HOW CAN I PAY?

    You can conveniently pay via instant transfer, credit card, PayPal and PayPal invoicing. To do this, simply select the desired payment method at the checkout. You can find further information about payment and the ordering process under Order & Payment.

  • HOW DO I REDEEM A VOUCHER?

    You enter a voucher code in the shopping cart. There you will find a field labeled “Redeem online voucher”.

  • USER ACCOUNT: I FORGOT MY PASSWORD. WHAT NOW?

    That's easy. You can have a new one sent to you via the link: “Forgot password”.

  • WHERE CAN I CHANGE MY ADDRESS?

    You can change your registered address at any time under “my account” -> “my data”. Adding alternative delivery addresses is possible in the checkout.

  • HOW CAN I DELETE MY USER ACCOUNT?

    Please call us or send us an email.
