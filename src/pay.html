Payment methods

HOW

You can pay with us using Sofortüberweisung, credit card, PayPal and PayPal invoicing.

SECURITY

Like all modern shops, your payment details are secured by an SSL security server and protected from unauthorized access.

SECURITY CODE

You can find the security code (or ccv) of your credit card next to or on the signature field. It is usually three digits long. For American Express it is on the front and has 4 digits.

INVOICE

Our weight information is net (i.e. without packaging).
Meat is a natural product and the portioned pieces are cut by hand every morning by experienced butchers. We strive to always deliver exactly the quantity you ordered within a very small tolerance. If it is more, the price will of course remain the same for you.
